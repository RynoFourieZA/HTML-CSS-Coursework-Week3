Welcome

Smart Bakers is a start-up bakery that is located in Zoar, Western Cape. Smart Bakers expects to catch the interest of a regular loyal customer base with its quirky and fun corporate image, and unusual flavour cupcakes. The company aims to dominate the market in the Western Cape due to the owner’s industry experience and the lack of competition in the province.
